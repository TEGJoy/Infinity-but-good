Frequently asked questions

Reporter Aurelia Ayasato asked The Phoenix some questions, these are answered below.

Question #1

How did you guys get together?

BM: While travelling, we gradually found each other in different cities. We all became good friends soon afterwards.

Question #2

Blue melody, you're finally back after the accident?

small silence..

BM: Yeah, I'm sorry for all my fans who had to wait for so long. It's been a rough few years, but I'm happy to finally play for you all again.
